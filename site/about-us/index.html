About us

Welcome to our multi-specialty healthcare center, a 125-bed hospital established in 1989 by Dr. S. Palaniswamy and Dr. P. Manimekalai. Our founders’ vision was to create a medical facility that provides a wide range of high-quality healthcare services at affordable costs. Over the past three decades, we’ve grown into one of Chennai’s leading healthcare institutions, known for our commitment to excellence and patient-centered care.

Our Location and Certifications

We are conveniently located in the heart of Chennai, making our hospital easily accessible to patients from across the city and beyond. Our location is a vital part of our mission, allowing us to serve a diverse community with a wide range of healthcare needs. Our commitment to quality and safety is underscored by our ISO 9001-2015 certification, which reflects our dedication to continuous improvement and operational excellence.

Additionally, our hospital has received multiple prestigious accreditations. We are NABH (National Accreditation Board for Hospitals & Healthcare Providers) accredited, a testament to our high standards in hospital management and clinical care. We also hold NABH accreditations for Nursing Excellence and Blood Bank services, ensuring that our nursing staff and blood bank operate at the highest levels of quality and safety. Our NABL (National Accreditation Board for Testing and Calibration Laboratories) accreditation demonstrates our laboratory’s commitment to accurate and reliable diagnostic services.

Comprehensive Medical and Surgical Services

As a multi-specialty healthcare center, we offer a broad spectrum of medical and surgical services for both children and adults. Our hospital is equipped with advanced technology for diagnostic and therapeutic purposes, ensuring that we can meet a variety of healthcare needs. From routine check-ups to complex surgical procedures, our facility is designed to provide comprehensive care in a single location.

Our team of medical and surgical professionals is highly skilled, with extensive experience in handling a wide range of medical conditions. Our specialists come from various fields, including cardiology, orthopedics, neurology, gastroenterology, pediatrics, and more. This diversity allows us to offer specialized care tailored to each patient’s unique needs. Our surgical team is equipped to perform intricate procedures with precision and care, utilizing the latest techniques to ensure the best outcomes.

Our Team and Patient Care

At our hospital, patient care is at the heart of everything we do. Our medical and surgical teams are supported by dedicated nurses, technicians, and support staff who work tirelessly to ensure a holistic healing experience for every patient. Our nursing staff is trained to provide compassionate and attentive care, ensuring that patients feel comfortable and supported throughout their stay.

We believe in a collaborative approach to healthcare, where every team member contributes to the patient’s well-being. Our technicians operate advanced diagnostic equipment to provide accurate and timely results, while our support staff ensures that the hospital environment is clean, safe, and welcoming. Together, we create an atmosphere where patients can focus on healing and recovery.

Our Commitment to the Community

Beyond our hospital walls, we are committed to serving the broader community. We engage in various outreach programs and health education initiatives to promote wellness and preventive care. Our goal is to make quality healthcare accessible to all, regardless of background or financial means.

Thank you for choosing our hospital for your healthcare needs. We look forward to providing you with the highest level of care and ensuring that your experience with us is positive and healing. If you have any questions or require assistance, our team is always here to help.

Kumaran Hospitals is a 125 bedded super speciality & multi-speciality healthcare center established in the year 1989 by Dr. S. Palaniswamy and Dr. P. Manimekalai to provide a wide spectrum of high quality medical services at affordable costs.
